This section covers the main bodies of the Solar System: the Sun, the Moon, the planets, and small bodies such as dwarf planets, comets, and meteors. These are the nearest and most accessible objects to observe, and they form the starting point of the learning path, providing the foundation for the deep-sky observing and astrophotography that follow.
