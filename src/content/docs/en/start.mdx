This site is aimed at readers of Chinese and compiles the foundational concepts, common workflows, and reference indexes for astronomical observation and astrophotography. You don't need to read through the table of contents in order; you can pick an entry point based on your goal first, then fill in the related concepts.

## Content Organization

The site is divided into two topic areas and one index area:

- **Astronomy**: coordinates and time, the Solar System, stars and deep-sky objects, cosmology, observing conditions, and observation planning.
- **Astrophotography**: optics and exposure, sensors, capture control, calibration frames, stacking, post-processing workflows, and remote imaging platforms.
- **Reference**: glossary, object catalogs, observatories, software, learning paths, and external resources.

## Three Categories of Entry Points

### Astronomy

The foundations cover [celestial coordinates](/en/astronomy/foundations/celestial-coordinates/), [time systems](/en/astronomy/foundations/time-systems/), and [magnitude](/en/astronomy/foundations/magnitude/). After that you can read about the [Solar System](/en/astronomy/solar-system/planets/), [stellar physics](/en/astronomy/deep-sky/stellar-physics/), [deep-sky objects](/en/astronomy/deep-sky/nebulae/), and a [cosmology overview](/en/astronomy/cosmology/overview/). Before heading out to observe, it is recommended that you first check the [observing conditions](/en/astronomy/observing/conditions/) and [observation planning](/en/astronomy/observing/planning/).

### Astrophotography

The photography section begins with [optics](/en/astrophotography/fundamentals/optics/), [exposure](/en/astrophotography/fundamentals/exposure/), [sensors](/en/astrophotography/fundamentals/sensors/), and [signal-to-noise ratio](/en/astrophotography/fundamentals/snr/), then moves on to [wide-angle](/en/astrophotography/capture/widefield/), [deep-sky](/en/astrophotography/capture/dso-basics/), [planetary](/en/astrophotography/capture/planetary/), and [narrowband](/en/astrophotography/capture/narrowband/) imaging. The data processing section covers [calibration frames](/en/astrophotography/calibration/calibration-frames/) and the [post-processing workflow](/en/astrophotography/processing/workflow/).
