This section covers the post-processing of astrophotography data, including calibration and stacking, the ordered workflow from a linear master to a finished image, and key techniques such as gradient removal, color, stretching, deconvolution, noise reduction, and star reduction. It sits between acquisition and the finished image, the stage in which raw FITS data is turned into a final picture.
